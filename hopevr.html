    • Hope: VR Escape Room Game

    Overview

    Hope is a virtual reality escape room game full of different puzzles. An individual grappling with various life challenges is trapped in their
    own room, unable to move on, as the game delves into their poignant journey. As they explore their surroundings, they uncover fragments
    of realisation, of their past, hidden clues and forgotten memories. They embark on a quest to escape their self-imposed isolation
    and break free from the shackles of their dark mind.

    Players have to step into the protagonist's world and experience their emotions and explore their room,
    uncovering clues that come to realisation and reveal their past and motivations. Players have to solve different types
    of puzzles that are deeply intertwined with the protagonist's memories and represent their journey towards self-discovery and acceptance.
