    Overview

    My passion is to become a UI/UX designer who loves creating beautiful and user-friendly
    interfaces for the web. Using design to solve real-world problems and improve people's lives
    is something I'm passionate about. My app, "The Mars Studio", is a portfolio of my work.
    It showcases my skills and experience in UI design for the web. It is my hope that by sharing
    my portfolio I will be able to share my story and interest in becoming a UI designer.

    More Details on the Project

    As part of our graded assignment, we were tasked with creating our own portfolio design for a
    mobile device. Only little information was given as this is solely part of our creativity to create a
    user friendly mobile application for yourself. As this is about myself, I added a lot of things that
    are related to being like, the colors, the small details of planets and stars and much more. I wanted
    to create something that would reflect my personality, so I decided to include a lot of personal touches.
    I also added a few cool animations to make the interface more interesting and interactive.
