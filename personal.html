    • Personal Poster

    brand story

    For this look, I researched the styles of various artists and worked on the aesthetics of the vintage
    look that I got inspired by online. This poster is based on a retro vibe. I used various color schemes
    and typography to get a cohesive design. I chose the same colour scheme as my logo to make everything consistent.
    Since I like anything with stars and planets and all those unique shapes, I also placed them on the poster as
    well. In addition, I had to think about the overall layout of the poster. This is an equally crucial factor when
    it comes to delivering a message as it is about ourselves.
