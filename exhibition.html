    • Singapore Exhibition Poster

    brand story

    I was tasked to create an exhibition poster about Chinese culture in Singapore. We were given
    little information and it is based on our own creativity. I did some researched and gathered information
    and inspiration to come up with a design. I used vibrant colors to represent the Chinese culture.
    I also included symbols that represent the Chinese culture. Lastly, I added a touch of modern elements to make
    the poster more visually appealing.
