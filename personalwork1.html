    Overview

    Back in 2019, I was an intern at Tribal Studioz, a robotics company. They were in need of a proper website to showcase
    their products and services. I was tasked with creating a website that would be user-friendly and easy to navigate.
    I decided to use WordPress to create the website. I also used Elementor to design the website. The website was
    designed to be responsive and mobile-friendly. The website was also designed to be SEO-friendly. As they have a lot of services
    to display, I decided to create a minimalist design to not overwhelmed users. As their logo is originally a red and white,
    I decided to follow the theme. It was an opportunity for me to explore and learn more using WordPress.
