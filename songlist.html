    • Song List App

    brand story

    As part of our lessons, we were required to create this song list application. The app was
    designed with a purpose of storing songs and allowing users to delete or remove songs as they
    see fit. The app also offers users the ability to search for songs. The app also includes a
    user-friendly interface, allowing users to easily navigate and add new songs to their list.

    key feature

    User be able to add songs, remove and search

    User be able to rate the songs
