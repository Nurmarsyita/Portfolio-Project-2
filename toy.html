    • Little Toy Kingdom

    Overview

    I enjoyed creating this website design for a kids toys website for parents to buy their children's
    toys. I included different soft colors that kids mostly like because they love bright natural colors.
    I also made sure to include the pictures of the toys, so that the parents can know what they're buying.
    I also added a section for customer reviews, so that parents can read reviews from other parents who
    have bought the products.
